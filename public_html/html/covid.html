COVID-19 Information

Following recommendations by Governor Inslee and the Department of Corrections, Prison Pet Partnership has suspended all programming. Staff offices are currently closed. Please follow this page and our social media for updates.

Service Dog Program

We are currently closing all service dog applications and are not accepting any new applicants until 2021 or until further notice.

New service dog placements are on hold until October 1, 2020 or until further notice.

We are not accepting owner-surrenders or service puppies until September 1, 2020 or until further notice.

All current service dogs-in-training are in foster homes outside of the facility.

Boarding & Grooming

All boarding and grooming services are currently suspended through July 5, 2020 or until further notice. Please continue to check this page for updates after July 5. We will contact groom clients when we are ready to reschedule.

Volunteers

All volunteer programming is suspended. Current Red Badge volunteers are not allowed within the facility until further notice, per WA Department of Corrections.

New volunteer applications are on hold and will not be processed until September 1, 2020 at the earliest.

Contact

Staff offices are closed until further notice. To reach a Prison Pet Partnership staff member, please email us at info@prisonpetpartnership.org and your message will be forwarded to the appropriate department. Our phone messages are checked periodically. Please be patient!
