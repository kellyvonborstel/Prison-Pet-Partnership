Donate & Support

Donate

There are three easy ways to donate to Prison Pet Partnership. Visit Small Token to give a gift in honor of someone, download our Mail-in Form to send a donation by mail, or simply enter your donation amount below.

Support PPP While you shop!

Fred Meyer shoppers: Go to fredmeyer.com and use our NPO #85251 to link your Fred Meyer rewards card to help Prison Pet Partnership earn a donation!

Amazon: Prison Pet Partnership gets 0.5% of all your eligible purchases every time you shop at Amazon. Log on at smile.amazon.com.

Other Ways to Support PPP

Walk for a dog with Wooftrax! Get the app from wooftrax.com and when you walk your dog, start the app! Prison Pet Partnership gets a donation based on the miles walked/run with your dog!

Create a Facebook Fundraiser for your birthday or anytime to encourage your community to support PPP. Visit Facebook Fundraiser to easily create one.

Have a unique idea or way to support PPP? Email Sarah at sarah.anderson@doc1.wa.gov to connect.

Wish List

The Wish List indicates the supplies that the program most needs. If you would like to support us by sending a donation of supplies or materials, please choose something from our list and call us for shipping instructions. You can also purchase a gift card from office and pet food suppliers online and mail that as a donation. Or to make donating even easier, view our Amazon Wish List where you can purchase items online and they will be shipped directly to us.
